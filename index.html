Together We can Save Hunger

Annadann Foundation, an NGO with the objective to provide food for the economically weaker sections of our society, is an NGO in India directly benefitting over 15,00,000 children and their families every year, through more than 400 live welfare projects on healthcare, livelihood , malnutrition in over 2000 remote villages and slums across 25 states of India.

About Us

Annadana is a Club in NMAMIT which manily focuses on donating extra food and ration to the near by NGOs, Old age homes etc in Karkala
